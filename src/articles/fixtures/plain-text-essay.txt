Reliable systems make their boundaries visible. A collector should record what it received, what it rejected, and why, without retaining sensitive source material.

Deterministic extraction is especially useful for plain-text essays and technical notes. Their structure is already meaningful, so normalization should preserve paragraph boundaries while removing only insignificant surrounding whitespace.

When the source is incomplete, the pipeline should mark that limitation explicitly. Discussion analysis can still proceed without pretending that article evidence was available.
